About Our Station

Studio Location: Siwan, Bihar – 841226, India

Radio Badal Bhojpuri is the official Bhojpuri music channel of Radio Badal, created exclusively for Bhojpuri music lovers around the world. As an extension of the Radio Badal network, this dedicated channel delivers the best of Bhojpuri music, devotional songs, folk melodies, evergreen classics, and the latest chartbusters—24 hours a day.

Radio Badal Bhojpuri proudly carries forward the trusted legacy of Radio Badal, offering a dedicated platform for Bhojpuri music and culture while connecting listeners across India and around the globe, 24×7.
